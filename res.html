Introduction

My research journey spans 13 years (2012 to present), starting from chemistry (organic synthesis, biochemistry, and analytical chemistry) in my undergraduate years of study. My postgraduate studies were in agricultural biotechnology in wine production. This interest escalated into further studies into multi-modal, holistic methods. These included using complementary analytical chemistry, sensory/consumer insights, and data analysis tools to derive insight.

Areas of research

My current research interets are in the applications of Data Science in the fields: Analytical Chemistry, Bibliometrics, Biotechnology, Oenology, and Sensory evaluation. I work on transdisciplinary approaches for insightful data analysis.

Contributions to the science community

I have contributed 13 scientific articles and two book chapters in my fields of application.
